Partners

The commercial prawn fishing industry does not work in isolation. The PPFA maintains relationships with government, service providers, environmental groups, First Nations and associations, and other industry associations.

    These relationships facilitate:
  • Improved communications with other user groups such as Sport Fishing Advisory Board (SFAB)
  • Improved communication and understanding of shared business interests with First Nations
  • Improved communications with environmental groups such as the Canadian Parks and Wilderness society (CPAWS)
  • Sharing of advocacy and market promotion interests through Industry Umbrella Associations such as the BC Seafood Alliance and the Chef’s Table Society
  • A shared resource management structure with DFO
  • Resource stewardship and eco-certification programs such as Marine Stewardship Certification (MSC), Monterey Bay Aquarium Recognition program or the Vancouver Aquarium
  • Commercial Fishing Industry Vessel Safety through FishSafe
